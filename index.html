Boyertown Technology Student Association

The Boyertown Area Senior High School chapter of the Technology Student Association, also known as BASH TSA, is a nationally renowned chapter in the 250,000+ member student STEM organization.

Over the past few years, BASH TSA has grown to break national records and gain recognition for the highest levels of community involvement, charitable fundraising, and competitive success. The chapter also offers a closely-knit and friendly environment at BASH which creates its own tools for success year after year. BASH TSA is not just a club, it's a family with shared interests and goals that represents Boyertown at the highest possible level.
